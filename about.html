About US

Association Bubblegum is a cultural organisation that was established in Johannesburg in 2015 and has been based in Geneva since 2024. We are a cultural platform and creative studio dedicated to nurturing and amplifying diverse creative voices. Operating at the intersection of publishing and creative production, we spotlight emerging talent, craft bold visual narratives, and shape contemporary culture through editorial content, campaigns, and collaborations.
