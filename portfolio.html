About US

DSTV World of more

BRIEF:
We were then tasked with creating a promo for Dstv that would land the message of MORE in away that was unique but still aligned to DStv’s look and feel.

APPROACH:
We brought DStv’s promise of a “world of more” to life through the personification of the brand— imagining it as a relatable, charming, and distinctly South African character who embodies versatility and abundance. Inspired by archetypes like the OG hustler or Gen Z jack-of-all-traits, the character reflects the many hats South Africans wear in their daily lives.The creative execution centred around a looping visual of a single character seamlessly morphing into different versions of themselves — from athlete to chef to fashionista — each transformation paired with voiceover narration highlighting DStv’s wide range of content offerings. A side screen visually reinforced this message, showcasing the platform’s diverse entertainment universe in a way that felt aspirational, inclusive, and dynamic.

Grants Together

BRIEF:
Grant’s tasked us with localising the global ‘How To Crew’ series — an inspiring digital campaign that explores real-life challenges through the lens of unconventional experts. Our brief was to deepen local relevance by creating content that resonates with South African audiences while staying true to the brand’s positioning, Together We Make It.The goal was to drive awareness, create culturally grounded assets, and maximise earned media through digital and on-the-ground activations that reflect the lived experiences and dynamics of local friendship crews.

APPROACH:
Inspired by themes of connection, community, and collective achievement, our concept explored what “Together We Make It” means within distinct local contexts. We identified and profiled two real-life crews — one in South Africa and one in Nigeria — each rooted in the passion points, industries, and cultural dynamics unique to their market.Through the creation of two locally resonant films, we celebrated the power of collaboration and shared success, showcasing how community and cooperation are central to navigating real-life challenges —and ultimately, how we go further together.

Spotify x Thebe Magugu

BRIEF:
Spotify approached us to conceptualise and execute a bold creative direction for Thebe Magugu’s takeover of the Fashion Forward playlist — marking the first time an African designer led the campaign. Tasked with building a visual and experiential world around Thebe’s Folklorics collection, our team brought together design, content, and live performance to celebrate the intersection of fashion, music, and African storytelling.

APPROACH:
We developed a cohesive campaign that spanned Spotify’s on-platform presence, out-of-home(OOH) executions, and a live showcase event. Our visual identity drew from the sonic duality of the playlist — weaving together Afro-foklore and acoustic elements — while the event activation grounded the global campaign in the energy and diversity of local cultural scene.

Adidas ZX

BRIEF:
adidas tasked us with conceptualising and executing a launch and seeding campaign for theZX silhouette, focused on driving organic reach without paid media support. The goal was to position ZX as a shoe that embodies innovation, progress, and unexpected design, while tapping into the "oddly satisfying" feeling of its unique tech and comfort. Targeted at a sporty, Gen Z audience, the campaign needed to feel offbeat, bold, and shareable — building hype, sparking conversation, and increasing brand awareness and product consideration in afresh, unconventional way.

APPROACH:
We developed an immersive, narrative-led seeding experience that embodied the “oddly satisfying” spirit of adidas ZX. Guests were invited into a surreal, art-meets-tech environment guided by a fictional voice assistant, Zxola, who led them through the space, revealing product in unexpected, playful ways.The experience blended visual storytelling, interactivity, and live music performance, encouraging content creation and social sharing — all designed todrive organic buzz, brand love, and cultural relevance without relying on paid media.
